BlockDonald's
BlockDonald’s is an add-on for Farmer’s Delight that brings iconic fast food into Minecraft. With this expansion, players can cook and enjoy classic burgers, crispy fries in several variations, chicken nuggets, refreshing drinks, and even experiment with new ingredients like cheese, and mustard. A brand new deep fryer block makes it possible to prepare these meals just like in a real fast food kitchen, adding more fun and variety to your cooking adventures
