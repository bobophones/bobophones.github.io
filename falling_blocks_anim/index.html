Falling Blocks Anim
This mod adds animation for falling blocks. Revives the mechanics of falling blocks by adding smooth rotation when falling. Sand, gravel, concrete and other blocks no longer just fall — they spin like real physical objects, creating a more dynamic picture of destruction.
